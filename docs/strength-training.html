Home Strength Routine

3 days/week · 2 sets per exercise · Dumbbells + Functional Trainer
Monday — Push Wednesday — Pull Friday — Legs & Core

💪 Day 1 — Push

Chest, Shoulders & Triceps
1
Dumbbell Bench Press ▶ YT
2 × 10–12 reps 60–90s rest Dumbbells
2
Cable Chest Fly (Low to High) ▶ YT
2 × 12–15 reps 60s rest Functional Trainer
3
Dumbbell Shoulder Press ▶ YT
2 × 10–12 reps 60–90s rest Dumbbells
4
Cable Lateral Raise ▶ YT
2 × 12–15 reps 45s rest Functional Trainer
5
Cable Tricep Pushdown ▶ YT
2 × 12–15 reps 45s rest Functional Trainer
💡 Choose a weight where the last 2 reps feel challenging but form stays clean.

🔄 Day 2 — Pull

Back & Biceps
1
Cable Seated Row ▶ YT
2 × 10–12 reps 60–90s rest Functional Trainer
2
Cable Lat Pulldown ▶ YT
2 × 10–12 reps 60–90s rest Functional Trainer
3
Single-Arm Dumbbell Row ▶ YT
2 × 10–12 reps each side 60s rest Dumbbells
4
Cable Face Pull ▶ YT
2 × 15 reps 45s rest Functional Trainer
5
Dumbbell Hammer Curl ▶ YT
2 × 12 reps 45s rest Dumbbells
💡 Face pulls protect shoulder health — don't skip them. Keep a neutral spine on all rows.

🦵 Day 3 — Legs & Core

Quads, Hamstrings & Core
1
Dumbbell Goblet Squat ▶ YT
2 × 12 reps 90s rest Dumbbells
2
Cable Romanian Deadlift ▶ YT
2 × 10–12 reps 90s rest Functional Trainer
3
Dumbbell Reverse Lunge ▶ YT
2 × 10 reps each leg 60s rest Dumbbells
4
Cable Woodchop (High to Low) ▶ YT
2 × 12 reps each side 45s rest Functional Trainer
5
Plank Hold ▶ YT
2 × 30–45 seconds 45s rest Bodyweight
💡 Hinge from the hips (not lower back) on the RDL. On lunges, keep your front knee over your second toe.

📋 General Guidelines

📅
Schedule Any 3 non-consecutive days work. Mon / Wed / Fri is ideal.
📈
Progressive Overload Every 1–2 weeks, add a little weight or squeeze out an extra rep to keep growing.
🥩
Protein Aim for 0.7–1g per lb of bodyweight daily to support lean muscle.
⬆️
Level Up Once 2 sets feel easy, increase weight first — then consider adding a 3rd set.

"The last 2 reps of every set should be hard. If they're not, the weight is too light."
